Our services

Instead of spending huge sums to acquire software, hire and train staff, you can contract us to handle all your accounting, tax, compliance and recruitment tasks for far less than you would have spent handling it in-house.

JohnPearl Consulting (JPC) operates as an extension of (or substitute for) your internal accounts staff. We perform a variety of financial and accounting services, and offer you the maximum flexibility by allowing you to rely on us for any of the services that we offer as needed. The following sections provide details regarding some of the services that you'll have at your disposal should you choose JohnPearl Consulting (JPC) as your financial services provider.

Accounting Services

Our financial experts prepare your financial records for you.

Our range of Accounting Services include:
  • Accounts Department Setup
  • Financial statements preparation, analysis and interpretation
  • Payroll services (Structuring, Preparation, setup, Payroll Monthly run)
  • Budgeting
  • Business Plan Preparation and Review
  • Qualified Accountants to handle your Accounting tasks remotely
